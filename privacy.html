中文（繁體）

歡迎使用 CrossScan。我們非常重視您的隱私。本隱私權政策說明 CrossScan 應用程式如何處理您的資訊。

我們不收集的資訊

CrossScan 不會收集、儲存或上傳以下任何資訊：

  • 個人身份資訊（姓名、電子郵件、電話號碼等）
  • 位置資訊
  • 聯絡人資訊
  • 任何裝置識別碼（除廣告用途外，見下方說明）

本機儲存的資訊

CrossScan 會將掃描記錄僅儲存於您的裝置上。這些資料不會上傳到任何伺服器，也不會與第三方分享。您可以隨時在應用程式內刪除這些記錄。

相機使用

CrossScan 使用相機權限僅用於掃描 QR 碼與條碼。相機畫面僅在裝置上即時處理，不會被儲存或傳輸。

廣告與第三方服務

CrossScan 使用 Google AdMob 顯示廣告。AdMob 可能會使用裝置廣告識別碼（AD_ID）來提供個人化或非個人化廣告。您可以透過裝置設定重置或限制廣告追蹤：

  • iOS：設定 → 隱私權 → 追蹤 → 關閉「允許 App 要求追蹤」
  • Android：設定 → Google → 廣告 → 重設廣告 ID

Google AdMob 的隱私權政策請參閱：https://policies.google.com/privacy

English

Welcome to CrossScan. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how CrossScan handles your information.

What We Don't Collect

CrossScan does not collect, store, or upload any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Device identifiers (except for advertising, see below)

Locally Stored Data

CrossScan saves scan history only on your device. This data is never uploaded to any server or shared with third parties. You can delete records at any time within the app.

Camera Usage

CrossScan uses the camera solely for scanning QR codes and barcodes. The camera feed is processed in real-time on your device only and is never stored or transmitted.

Advertising & Third-Party Services

CrossScan uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use the device advertising identifier (AD_ID) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can reset or limit ad tracking through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Reset advertising ID

Google AdMob's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
